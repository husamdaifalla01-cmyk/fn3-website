# FTC Disclosure Templates — DriveCredit (mintbrooks.com)

**Last updated:** 2026-04-05
**Applicable law:** FTC Endorsement Guides, 16 CFR Part 255
**Platform:** TikTok, Instagram Reels

---

## Core Legal Position

Mintbrooks.com is an **affiliate marketing site**. It is **NOT a lender, broker, or financial advisor**. It earns advertising commissions when users click through and complete applications with third-party financial product providers. Every piece of content must make this relationship clear.

---

## 1. Short-Form Caption Disclosures (Copy-Paste Ready)

Use ONE of these at the **beginning** of every caption — not buried at the end or after a "see more" fold.

### Educational Content

```
#ad | Mintbrooks.com earns a commission if you apply through our link. We're not a lender — just sharing options we've researched. Not financial advice.
```

### Testimonial-Style / Story Content

```
#ad | I may earn a commission if you apply through my link. Mintbrooks.com is an affiliate site, not a lender. Your experience may vary. Not financial advice.
```

### Direct CTA ("Link in Bio" Style)

```
#ad | Paid affiliate link. Mintbrooks.com earns a commission from the lender if you apply. We don't lend money or guarantee approval. Terms set by lender. Not financial advice.
```

### Ultra-Short (Absolute Minimum — Use Only When Caption Space Is Critically Limited)

```
#ad | Affiliate link — I earn a commission. Not a lender. Not financial advice.
```

## 2. Long-Form Disclosure (Video Descriptions / Link-in-Bio Pages)

Place this on any landing page, link-in-bio page, or expanded video description:

```
DISCLOSURE: Mintbrooks.com is an independently operated affiliate marketing website.
We are NOT a lender, loan broker, or financial advisor. We do not make credit decisions,
set interest rates, or guarantee approval for any product.

When you click a link on this site or in our content and complete an application with
a third-party provider, we may receive an advertising commission at no additional cost
to you. This compensation may influence which products we feature and where they appear,
but it does not affect our editorial opinions.

All financial products are offered by third-party lenders and are subject to their own
terms, conditions, and eligibility requirements. Product availability, rates, and terms
vary by state and applicant qualifications.

Nothing on this site constitutes financial advice. Consult a licensed financial
professional before making credit decisions.
```

## 3. In-Video Text Overlay Guidance

The FTC requires disclosures to be **clear and conspicuous**. Captions alone are not enough if the video itself contains claims or endorsements.

### Requirements

- **Placement:** Lower third of the screen, visible for at least 3 seconds at the start of the video
- **Font size:** Large enough to read on a mobile screen (minimum 24pt equivalent)
- **Contrast:** White text on dark semi-transparent background, or black text on light background
- **Timing:** Must appear **before or simultaneous with** the first product mention, not after

### Recommended Overlay Text

**Standard:**
```
Paid affiliate link | Not a lender | Not financial advice
```

**If making income/savings claims:**
```
#AD | Affiliate — I earn a commission | Results not guaranteed
```

**If referencing a specific product (e.g., Yendo):**
```
#AD | Affiliate link to [Product] | I'm not the lender
```

### What NOT to Do with Overlays

- Do not use tiny text that blends into the background
- Do not flash it for under 2 seconds
- Do not place it only in the last frame
- Do not rely solely on a hashtag (#ad) buried in a caption below the fold

## 4. State Availability Disclaimer

Financial products are not available in all states. Include this when promoting a specific product:

### Short Version (Caption)
```
Availability varies by state. Check lender site for details.
```

### Long Version (Landing Page)
```
Product availability, terms, and eligibility requirements vary by state and are
determined solely by the third-party lender. Mintbrooks.com does not guarantee
that any product is available in your state. Please verify availability and
terms directly with the lender before applying.
```

## 5. Do's and Don'ts Checklist

### DO

- [x] Place #ad or "Paid affiliate" at the **very start** of every caption
- [x] Include an in-video text overlay disclosure on every video that mentions a product
- [x] State clearly that Mintbrooks is NOT a lender
- [x] Add "Not financial advice" to every post
- [x] Use the word "commission" or "earn" to explain the financial relationship
- [x] Include state availability language when referencing specific products
- [x] Disclose on every platform separately (TikTok caption AND IG caption)
- [x] Keep disclosure language before the "see more" fold on Instagram

### DON'T

- [ ] Guarantee approval ("You WILL get approved" — never say this)
- [ ] Promise specific credit limits, rates, or terms you cannot control
- [ ] Claim to be a lender, broker, or financial institution
- [ ] Bury the #ad hashtag among 20 other hashtags at the end
- [ ] Use only verbal disclosure in video without text overlay
- [ ] Imply a personal relationship with the lender ("my partner Yendo")
- [ ] Make income claims without substantiation
- [ ] Use fake urgency ("Only 3 spots left!" when there is no limit)
- [ ] Show fake application screenshots or fabricated approval screens
- [ ] Use testimonials without clearly stating "Results not typical" or "Your experience may vary"
- [ ] Remove or obscure disclosures after initial posting
- [ ] Re-use someone else's testimonial as if it were your own experience

## 6. Platform-Specific Notes

### TikTok
- TikTok has a built-in "Paid partnership" label — use it in addition to caption disclosure, not instead of it
- TikTok captions have a 4,000-character limit — no excuse to skip disclosures
- Pin a comment with the full disclosure if caption space is tight

### Instagram Reels
- Use the "Paid partnership" tag when available
- Disclosure must appear **before** the "...more" truncation point
- Add long-form disclosure to the link-in-bio landing page

- **State advertising laws:** Vary. Do not make state-specific product claims without verifying availability.
