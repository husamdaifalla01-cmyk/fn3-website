## SCRIPT 7: "The Rejection Loop"

**Angle:** Empathy-first. Emotional weight of serial denial. This is NOT about the product -- it is about the person. The car-secured card enters as the exit door from a cycle, not as a pitch.
**Hook Type:** Emotional storytelling
**Duration Target:** 45-55 seconds
**Platforms:** TikTok + IG Reels

---

### HOOK (0-3s)

**Say exactly:**
"I got denied seven times in one year. Seven. And every single one made it harder to get the next one."

**On-screen text overlay:**
`denied 7 times in one year`

**Camera:** Extreme close-up. Just your face. No smile. Eyes slightly down, then look up at the lens on "seven." This is a confessional.

---

### BODY (3-35s)

**Say exactly:**

*[Voice low, measured. Not performing -- remembering.]*
"Here's what nobody tells you about getting denied. Each application is a hard inquiry. Each hard inquiry drops your score 5 to 10 points. So you apply, you get denied, your score goes down. You wait a couple weeks, try somewhere else, denied again, score drops again. You're not getting rejected because you're bad with money. You're getting rejected because the last rejection made you look worse."

*[Pause. One beat of silence. Then shift -- sit up straighter, voice gets firmer.]*
"That's the rejection loop. And I lived in it for a year before I found the exit."

*[New energy. Not salesy -- resolved.]*
"The exit is a card that doesn't run your credit at all. It looks at your car. If you own a car with equity, that equity becomes your credit line. $500 to $10,000 on a real Visa. The check is a soft pull. Your score doesn't move. If you don't qualify, it's like it never happened. No new hard inquiry. No damage. No rejection letter in the mail."

**On-screen text overlays (timed):**
- 3s: `each denial = hard inquiry = lower score`
- 12s: `the rejection loop`
- 20s: `the exit: car-secured credit card`
- 28s: `soft pull = no score damage`

**Camera directions:**
- 0-20s: Close-up talking head. Minimal movement. Let the words do the work.
- 20-35s: Pull back to medium shot OR cut to a different angle (same room, different position). The visual shift signals "here's the solution."

## SCRIPT 8: "I Ran the Numbers"

**Angle:** Pure math. Zero emotion. This is for the analytical viewer who doesn't trust feelings -- they trust spreadsheets. Hormozi's "show the math" framework. The numbers do the selling.
**Hook Type:** Logic/data
**Duration Target:** 40-55 seconds
**Platforms:** TikTok + IG Reels

---

### HOOK (0-3s)

**Say exactly:**
*[Holding a piece of paper or phone with numbers on it. Look at it, then look at camera.]*
"I ran the numbers on secured cards vs. car-secured cards and the math is embarrassing."

**On-screen text overlay:**
`I ran the numbers. It's not even close.`

**Camera:** Medium shot. Desk or table visible. Paper/whiteboard/phone in hand. "I did the homework" energy.

---

### BODY (3-40s)

**Say exactly:**

*[Set the paper down. Use hands to illustrate. Teacher mode.]*
"Scenario. You have bad credit. You want to build it. You have two paths."

*[Hold up one finger.]*
"Path A: Traditional secured card. You put down $300 cash. They give you a $300 limit. You spend $150 a month on groceries -- that's 50 percent utilization. FICO penalizes anything over 30 percent. So even though you're paying on time every month, your score barely moves. After 12 months you've paid fees, your deposit is locked up, and your score went up maybe 20 points."

*[Hold up two fingers.]*
"Path B: Car-secured card. You own a car worth $8,000. You put down zero dollars. They give you a $3,000 credit line based on your car's equity. You spend the same $150 a month. That's 5 percent utilization. FICO loves that. Your score climbs 50 to 80 points in the same 12 months. You saved the $300 deposit. And your car? You're still driving it."

*[Pause. Let that compute.]*
"Same person. Same spending. Same 12 months. One path gives you 20 points. The other gives you 50 to 80. The only difference is which card you picked."

**On-screen text overlays (timed):**
- 3s: `Path A: Secured Card`
- 5s: `$300 deposit -> $300 limit`
- 8s: `$150 spend = 50% utilization`
- 12s: `12 months = ~20 point gain`
- 16s: `Path B: Car-Secured Card`
- 18s: `$0 deposit -> $3,000 limit`
- 21s: `$150 spend = 5% utilization`
- 25s: `12 months = ~50-80 point gain`
- 32s: `same person. same spending. different card.`

**Camera directions:**
- Stationary medium shot the whole time. No walking. No movement. Let the math hold attention.
- If you have a whiteboard or large notepad, write the two paths side by side as you talk. Visual math is powerful.
- Alternative: use split-screen text overlays if no whiteboard available.

---

### CTA (40-50s)

**Say exactly:**

"The car-secured card is through a company called Yendo. I share it through Mintbrooks, and I may earn a commission if you apply through my link. Full disclosure. But the math is the math. Link in my bio. Soft pull. Five minutes. See what your car's equity gets you."

**On-screen text overlay:**
`Paid affiliate link | Not a lender | Not financial advice`

## SCRIPT 9: "State Check"

**Angle:** Urgency and scarcity. The card isn't available everywhere. Your state might lose access. Your car is depreciating. Two clocks are ticking. This is the "act now" video -- but grounded in real facts, not fake countdown timers.
**Hook Type:** Scarcity/urgency
**Duration Target:** 35-45 seconds
**Platforms:** TikTok + IG Reels

---

### HOOK (0-3s)

**Say exactly:**
*[Look at camera with slight concern, like delivering breaking news.]*
"This credit card is only available in 37 states right now. And that number used to be higher."

**On-screen text overlay:**
`only available in 37 states (and shrinking)`

**Camera:** Close-up talking head. Serious tone. No smile.

---

### BODY (3-30s)

**Say exactly:**

*[Walk toward camera slowly as you talk.]*
"Here's the thing about car-secured credit cards. They're new. The regulatory framework is still being built state by state. Right now, 37 states have it. But states review these programs periodically. Some have paused. Some are under review. There is no guarantee your state keeps it."

*[Stop walking. Face camera directly.]*
"And while you wait? Your car is losing value. Every car depreciates 15 to 20 percent per year. That $8,000 car is worth $6,500 next year. Your potential credit line shrinks with it. So you've got two clocks ticking -- state availability and vehicle depreciation."

*[Small head shake.]*
"I don't say this to pressure you. I say it because the math changes every month, and not in your favor."

**On-screen text overlays (timed):**
- 3s: `37 states currently eligible`
- 10s: `state programs under periodic review`
- 16s: `cars depreciate 15-20% per year`
- 22s: `two clocks: state access + car value`

**Camera directions:**
- 0-15s: Walking toward camera, medium shot
- 15-30s: Stationary close-up for the "two clocks" section. This needs to feel direct and urgent.
