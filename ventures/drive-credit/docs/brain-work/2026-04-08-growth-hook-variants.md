## Context
These 5 hook variants are ready for A/B testing once posting begins. Each targets a different psychological trigger. Post 1 version per video, track which hook drives highest watch-through + profile click rate.

---

## Hook Variant 1: The Rejection Callback
**Trigger:** Pain/identity
**Script (0–3s):**
> "Your bank didn't turn you down because you're irresponsible. They turned you down because they don't profit from helping you."

**Why it works:** Reframes the rejection as systemic, not personal. Builds trust immediately.
**Best for:** Audience: first-time credit seekers who feel shame about their score.

---

## Hook Variant 2: The Surprising Math
**Trigger:** Curiosity/self-interest
**Script (0–3s):**
> "If your car is worth $8,000 and you have no credit — you actually have $6,000 in available credit. Most people have no idea."

**Why it works:** Makes abstract value concrete. "I have that" is the thought.
**Best for:** Car owners who've never considered using equity.

---

## Hook Variant 3: The Insider Language
**Trigger:** Belonging/exclusivity
**Script (0–3s):**
> "There's a type of credit card that the biggest banks don't advertise — because it doesn't require them."

**Why it works:** Implies the viewer is about to learn something gatekept.
**Best for:** Conspiracy-adjacent audiences, users who distrust banks.

---

## Hook Variant 4: The Direct Challenge
**Trigger:** Challenge/proof
**Script (0–3s):**
> "Tell me your credit score and I'll tell you if this works for you in 10 seconds."

**Why it works:** Creates immediate engagement. Comment section fills with scores. Algorithm boost.
**Best for:** High-comment engagement goal, watch time optimization.

---

## Hook Variant 5: The Specificity Hook
**Trigger:** Specificity signals credibility
**Script (0–3s):**
> "A 2018 Honda Civic with 80k miles qualifies for a $4,200 credit card. With no credit check. Here's how."

**Why it works:** Hyper-specific examples feel real, not marketing. "That's my car" is the click.
**Best for:** Car-owner audience, middle-funnel (already knows about car equity concept).
