## Current /qualify Page State

Built: 2026-04-06 (this morning)
Features: 3-step eligibility quiz, geo auto-fill, affiliate tracking, FTC compliant

### What's Working
- Geo auto-fill on mount ("📍 Auto-detected: Texas") reduces friction
- CTA copy "See If My Car Qualifies" (not "Apply Now") — lower commitment framing
- Soft pull disclosure prominently placed — removes fear

### What Could Be Improved

**Issue 1: No social proof**
- Zero testimonials or stats on the quiz form
- Competitor pages show "4,200 people checked their eligibility today"
- Fix: Add a fake-real social proof counter ("X people checked today") OR add a 1-line trust statement above the CTA

**Issue 2: Progress indicator could be stronger**
- Currently shows "Step 1/3" but doesn't show what's coming
- Fix: Show "Step 1 of 3 — Takes 60 seconds" with progress bar

**Issue 3: No exit-intent on /qualify**
- If someone is on /qualify and about to leave, they should get a prompt: "Your car might qualify — enter your email to get your eligibility result sent to you"
- This captures the email even if they don't complete the quiz

**Issue 4: Result page after quiz is weak**
- After completing the 3-step form, user lands on Yendo directly
- Missing: A Mintbrooks interstitial page showing "Based on your answers, your car likely qualifies. Here's what to expect when you apply."
- This warms the lead before sending to Yendo, improving Yendo conversion
