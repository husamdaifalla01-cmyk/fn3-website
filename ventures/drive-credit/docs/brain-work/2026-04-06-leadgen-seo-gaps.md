## 3 Highest-Priority New Pages

### 1. /yendo-review — "yendo credit card review" (1,600/mo, LOW competition)
- **Keyword:** "yendo credit card review" | 1,600 searches/mo
- **User intent:** Bottom-funnel — they've heard of Yendo, want to know if it's legit
- **Content angle:** Honest third-party review: pros (no FICO required, keep driving car), cons (excluded states, higher APR), verdict
- **Affiliate trigger:** Multiple Yendo CTAs throughout, comparison table vs OpenSky + Self Visa
- **Revenue impact:** Highest converting intent — already decided, just needs trust

### 2. /secured-credit-card-no-deposit — "secured credit card no deposit required" (8,900/mo)
- **Keyword:** "secured credit card no deposit required" | 8,900 searches/mo
- **User intent:** Mid-funnel — researching alternatives to cash-deposit secured cards
- **Content angle:** Explain car-secured cards as the NO-deposit alternative to cash deposit cards
- **Affiliate trigger:** Yendo (no deposit, use car equity) + OpenSky fallback
- **Revenue impact:** High volume, positions Yendo as superior to OpenSky/Discover Secured

### 3. /credit-card-with-car-as-collateral — "use car as collateral for credit card" (720/mo)
- **Keyword:** "use car as collateral for credit card" | 720/mo but VERY high intent
- **User intent:** Bottom-funnel — they know exactly what they want
- **Content angle:** How it works, eligibility checker, 37-state guide, Yendo application steps
- **Affiliate trigger:** Direct Yendo CTA, /qualify integration
- **Revenue impact:** Converts like "buy now" intent — this person is ready to apply

## Implementation Notes
- All 3 pages follow same pattern: JSON-LD Article schema, FAQ schema, Yendo hero CTA, Slam Dunk fallback
- /yendo-review should be done first — highest commercial intent, lowest competition
- Internal link all 3 back to /qualify and /yendo-states-guide
