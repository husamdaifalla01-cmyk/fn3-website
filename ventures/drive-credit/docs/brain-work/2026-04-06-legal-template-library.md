## HOOKS (Pre-Approved)

These hook formulations have been reviewed for FTC compliance, UDAAP risk, and MaxBounty/Yendo affiliate terms.

### ✅ APPROVED Hooks

**Curiosity/Education hooks:**
- "The secret your car is hiding" ✅
- "What banks don't want you to know about car equity" ✅
- "Here's what happens when the bank says no" ✅
- "Why your credit score doesn't tell the whole story" ✅
- "The credit product built for people with bad credit" ✅

**Math/Logic hooks:**
- "I ran the numbers on bad credit financing" ✅ (as long as no specific rates claimed)
- "Here's how car equity lending actually works" ✅
- "The real cost of bad credit — and one way around it" ✅

**Problem/Solution hooks:**
- "Rejected for credit? Here's an alternative" ✅
- "Traditional banks won't touch you. These lenders will." ✅ (with "soft pull only" disclosure)
- "Your car might qualify you for credit your score can't" ✅

### ❌ PROHIBITED Hooks (do not use)
- "Get approved even with no credit" — implies guaranteed approval
- "No credit check required" — use "soft pull only" instead
- "Guaranteed credit card" — approval not guaranteed
- "Better than a bank" — comparative claim requires substantiation
- "Hack your credit score" — implies deceptive activity
- "Loophole" — regulators flag this word in credit advertising

### ❌ PROHIBITED CTAs
- "Apply now and get approved" — implies guaranteed approval
- "Click to get your credit card" — implies guaranteed product delivery
- "Fix your credit today" — implies credit repair service
- "Get approved in 60 seconds" — misleading timing claims
