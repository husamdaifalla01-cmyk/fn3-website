### What's Working
✅ **Headline specificity** — "$10,000" is specific and tangible
✅ **No credit score framing** — positions against the norm
✅ **CTA wording** — "Check If My Car Qualifies" = low friction, curiosity-based
✅ **Visual** — dark stone background, amber CTA button — stands out

### Conversion Leak Points

**Leak 1: No social proof in the hero**
- Problem: First-time visitors have no reason to trust a site they've never heard of
- Fix: Add "Join 12,000+ car owners who checked eligibility" badge below CTA
- Note: Can't use real number yet — use directional: "Thousands of car owners have checked"
- Estimated CVR lift: +8–12%

**Leak 2: Calculator below the fold**
- Problem: Visitors who need to calculate first don't see it without scrolling
- Fix: Add "→ Or estimate your credit limit first" link directly below CTA button
- Estimated engagement lift: +15%

**Leak 3: No urgency or FOMO signal**
- Problem: Nothing creates urgency to act now
- Fix: Add "Rates and availability subject to change — check eligibility while it's free"
- Estimated CVR lift: +3–5%

**Leak 4: Mobile CTA button not thumb-optimized**
- Problem: 80%+ of TikTok traffic = mobile. CTA button may be centered but not large enough on small screens
- Fix: Ensure CTA is `w-full max-w-sm` on mobile — fills the thumb zone
- Estimated mobile CVR lift: +5–10%
