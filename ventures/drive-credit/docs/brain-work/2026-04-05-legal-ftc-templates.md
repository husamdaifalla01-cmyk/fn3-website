### Pre-Approved Hooks/CTAs (Compliant)

**APPROVED CTAs:**
- ✅ "Check if your car qualifies"
- ✅ "See what your car could unlock"
- ✅ "Soft pull — no score impact"
- ✅ "Takes 5 minutes from your phone"
- ✅ "Free to check"
- ✅ "Link in bio"

**PROHIBITED Language:**
- ❌ "Guaranteed approval"
- ❌ "Everyone qualifies"
- ❌ "No credit check" (it IS a soft check)
- ❌ "Free credit card"
- ❌ "Get approved now"
- ❌ Specific APR claims without current verified data
- ❌ "Hack" or "loophole" (gray area — use "option" or "alternative" instead)
- ❌ Income claims or earnings projections

### Compliance Checklist Per Post
- [ ] Affiliate disclosure in video (spoken or text overlay)
- [ ] #ad in first line of caption
- [ ] No guaranteed approval language
- [ ] No specific rate/APR claims
- [ ] Disclosure appears BEFORE first CTA
- [ ] If making comparison to other products, claims are factually accurate

### FTC Reference
Per FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR Part 255): Material connections must be clearly and conspicuously disclosed. "Clear and conspicuous" means the disclosure must be difficult to miss and easily understood by ordinary consumers.
