`the day a bank teller laughed at my credit score`

## SCRIPT B: "Myth-Busting" — 3 Things Your Bank Won't Tell You

**Angle:** Authority/educator. Bust 3 specific myths fast. Pattern interrupt with the word "wrong" to keep retention. This is the "save this" video.
**Duration Target:** 20-25 seconds
**Tone:** Confident, slightly frustrated on behalf of the viewer, rapid-fire

---

### HOOK (0-3 seconds)

**Say exactly:**
"Three things your bank will never tell you about credit."

**On-screen text overlay:**
`3 things your bank won't tell you`

**Camera:** Medium talking head (chest up). Hold up 3 fingers. Direct eye contact.

---

### BODY (3-18 seconds)

**Say exactly:**
"One -- a higher credit limit is more important than paying on time. Your utilization ratio is what moves your score fastest. Two -- every application you submit is a hard pull that drops your score. They don't warn you before they run it. Three -- your car is collateral. There's a Visa card that skips your credit score entirely and uses your car's equity instead. Up to ten thousand dollars. Soft pull to check."

**Camera directions:**
- Talking head the entire time. No cuts. Rapid delivery.
- Hold up one finger on "One," two on "Two," three on "Three"
- On "Three" -- lean in slightly closer to the camera. This is the payoff.

**On-screen text overlays (timed, appear with each number):**
- 3s: `1. utilization > payment history`
- 8s: `2. every app = hard pull`
- 13s: `3. your car is collateral`

---

### CTA (18-24 seconds)

**Say exactly:**
"Affiliate link in bio. Five minutes. Soft pull. Go check what your car unlocks."

**On-screen text overlay:**
`link in bio | affiliate link | #ad`

**Camera:** Same frame. Point at camera, then point down.

## SCRIPT C: "Tutorial / How-To" — Check If Your Car Qualifies in 30 Seconds

**Angle:** Pure utility. Screen-record style walkthrough mixed with talking head. "Let me show you exactly how" energy. This is the "I'm doing it right now" video.
**Duration Target:** 18-23 seconds
**Tone:** Helpful, calm, step-by-step. Like a friend showing you something on their phone.

---

### HOOK (0-3 seconds)

**Say exactly:**
"Here's how to check if your car qualifies for a credit card in thirty seconds."

**On-screen text overlay:**
`how to check if your car qualifies (30 sec)`

**Camera:** Talking head, holding up phone toward camera.

---

### BODY (3-17 seconds)

**Say exactly:**
"Step one -- tap the link in my bio. It takes you straight to the check page. Step two -- enter your car's year, make, and model. Step three -- it runs a soft pull. That means your credit score is not affected at all. In about five minutes you'll see if you qualify and what your credit line could be. That's it. No deposit. No commitment. Just information."

**Camera directions:**

- 10-17 sec: Back to talking head for "That's it. No deposit. No commitment."

**On-screen text overlays (timed):**
- 3s: `step 1: tap link in bio`
