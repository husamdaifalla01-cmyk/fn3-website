## 4 HOOK STYLES TO TEST (Week 1-2 A/B)

Track which hook type drives the highest 3-second view retention + link clicks:

### Hook A: Curiosity/Secret
> "Most people with a car and bad credit don't know this exists..."
- Best for: Cold audiences, discovery phase
- Expected: High reach, moderate CTR

### Hook B: Problem/Agitation
> "The bank said no. Again. Here's what they don't tell you."
- Best for: ICP who've been rejected before
- Expected: Moderate reach, high CTR from qualified viewers

### Hook C: Proof/Result
> "I went from no credit to a $3,000 Visa card using my 2015 Civic."
- Best for: Skeptical audience needing social proof
- Expected: Lower reach initially, highest CTR and conversion

### Hook D: Tutorial/How-to
> "Step 1: Check if your state is eligible. Step 2: Get your car's value..."
- Best for: High-intent searchers who landed on content
- Expected: Lower views, highest completion rate

**Recommendation:** Post Hook B first (problem/agitation = highest ICP resonance for bad-credit audience). Track 48h metrics before deciding Week 2 primary hook.

## CONTENT PRODUCTION SOP (for Remotion)

1. Pick script from content calendar
2. Write 3-phase script: Hook (0-3s) / Value (3-20s) / CTA (20-30s)
3. Run `npx remotion render src/remotion/index.ts MintbrooksTikTok /tmp/[name].mp4`
4. Add TTS voiceover via CapCut or ElevenLabs
5. Add captions (auto-caption on TikTok is sufficient)
6. Post with caption + hashtags from posting-copy.md
