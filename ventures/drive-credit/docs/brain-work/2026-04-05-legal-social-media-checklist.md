# Mintbrooks — FTC Social Media Compliance Checklist (Final)
**Date:** 2026-04-05 | **Author:** General Counsel (Compliance lens)

## Pre-Post Checklist (Run Before Every TikTok/IG Post)

### REQUIRED on every post:
- [ ] Caption includes "#ad" or "#sponsored" in the FIRST 3 lines (before "more" expansion)
- [ ] Caption includes "#mintbrooks" (brand association)
- [ ] No claim that Mintbrooks IS a lender or credit card issuer
- [ ] No claim of guaranteed approval
- [ ] No specific APR or interest rate mentioned (Yendo's terms change — don't hard-code them)
- [ ] "Soft pull" language used instead of "no credit check" (more accurate)

### For videos with voiceover:
- [ ] If you say "no credit check," add "for initial eligibility" qualifier
- [ ] If you say "up to $10,000," add "depending on your car's value"
- [ ] Do NOT say "guaranteed" or "instant approval"

### State availability caveat:
- [ ] Do NOT say "available everywhere" — say "available in 36+ states"
- [ ] On the /links page, keep the state disclaimer visible

## Pre-Approved Phrases (Copy-Safe)

### ✅ USE THESE:
- "Check if you qualify — soft pull only"
- "See what your car is worth in credit"
- "Available in 36+ states"
- "No deposit required"
- "Visa credit card secured by your car"
- "Link in bio · #ad"

### ❌ AVOID THESE:
- "No credit check" (inaccurate — there IS a soft pull, and hard pull later)
- "Guaranteed approval"
- "100% approval rate"
- "Free money"
- "Instant approval"
- "Your credit doesn't matter at all"

## FTC Enforcement Memo (Key Points)
The FTC's 2023 updated Endorsement Guides require:
1. **Clear and conspicuous disclosure** — not buried, not in tiny text, not only in hashtags at the end
2. **Disclosure before the fold** — on TikTok/IG, "#ad" must appear WITHOUT expanding "more"
3. **Video disclosures** — verbal disclosure OR on-screen text (both preferred)
4. **No implied endorsement** — don't say "I use this" unless you genuinely do
