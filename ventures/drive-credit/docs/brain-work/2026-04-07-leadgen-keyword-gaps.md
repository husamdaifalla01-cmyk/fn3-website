## Top 3 Recommended Next Pages

### 1. /first-credit-card-bad-credit (P0)
- **Target keyword:** "first credit card bad credit" (40k/mo)
- **Intent:** User has bad credit and wants their FIRST credit card
- **Perfect ICP match** — first-time credit seekers are most likely to use car equity
- **Angle:** "Your first credit card with bad credit doesn't have to be a secured card with a deposit"

### 2. /credit-card-no-credit (P0) 
- **Target keyword:** "credit card for no credit" (35k/mo)
- **Intent:** Thin file — no credit history at all, not even bad credit
- **ICP match:** Young people, immigrants, people who've never had credit
- **Angle:** "No credit history? Your car is your history."

### 3. /secured-vs-unsecured-credit-card (P1)
- **Target keyword:** "secured card vs unsecured card" (25k/mo)
- **Intent:** Comparison — educational, high buying intent
- **Angle:** Position car-secured card as the "third option" — better than secured (no deposit), accessible unlike unsecured
