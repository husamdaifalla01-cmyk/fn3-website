# Product Spec: 27th SEO Page
**Date:** 2026-04-10 | **Dept:** Product

## Target Page: /finance/car-equity-loan-requirements
**Keyword:** "car equity loan requirements" — ~22k/mo, low competition
**Intent:** Informational → purchase (user researching qualification criteria)
**Funnel fit:** Direct Yendo CTA — "check if your car qualifies"

## Why This Page
- High "can I qualify?" search intent = pre-qualified traffic
- Yendo's core differentiator is using car equity without surrendering keys
- Bridges existing /qualify page with top-of-funnel search

## Page Structure
1. **Hero**: "What Do You Need to Qualify for a Car Equity Loan?" (H1)
2. **Requirements table**: Vehicle year (2000+), title ownership, mileage (<200k), state availability
3. **Yendo requirements explainer**: Soft pull, no income minimum, insurance requirement
4. **Compare: Yendo vs traditional title loans** (keep car vs surrender keys)
5. **3-step qualification process**
6. **State availability checker** (link to /yendo-states-guide)
7. **FAQ schema**: 6 questions (min credit score, income req, loan amount, speed, impact on driving, renewal)
8. **CTA**: "Check if you qualify" → /qualify

- Secondary: Slam Dunk (for those who don't qualify on vehicle age)
