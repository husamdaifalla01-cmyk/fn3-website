## New Feature Spec: FAQ Sections on Guide Pages

### Why
- FAQ schema markup → Google rich snippets → more SERP real estate
- Answers objections that prevent clicks
- Lower bounce rate (people find answers, trust increases)

### Which pages (priority order)
1. `/credit-card-500-credit-score` — high commercial intent
2. `/credit-card-no-deposit` — high commercial intent  
3. `/no-credit-history-credit-card` — broad but converts

### FAQ format
3-5 Q&As per page, using real questions from Google's "People also ask":
- "What is the minimum credit score for Yendo?"
- "Does Yendo do a hard credit check?"
- "What cars qualify for Yendo?"
- "Can I use Yendo if I still have a car loan?"
- "What states is Yendo available in?"
