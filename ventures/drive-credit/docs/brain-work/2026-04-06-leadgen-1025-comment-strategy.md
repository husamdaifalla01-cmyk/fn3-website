## The Organic Lead Channel Nobody Uses
Most affiliates drive traffic via TikTok video → bio link → site.
The underused lever: **comment engagement on COMPETITOR videos**.

Bad credit TikTok is a real niche. People ask "how do I fix my credit?" in comments on finance videos. These are warm leads who've already self-identified.

## Target Videos to Engage On

### Search Strategy (TikTok search bar)
Search these exact phrases — filter to "This week":
1. "bad credit credit card"
2. "car secured credit"
3. "how to build credit from scratch"
4. "credit score 500"
5. "how to get approved for credit card"

### Engagement Rules
- Only comment on INFORMATIONAL videos (not competitors' affiliate content)
- Never directly mention Mintbrooks in comments on other pages (spam risk)
- Instead: answer the question genuinely, establish expertise, then people find us via profile

### Comment Templates (Non-Promotional)

**Template A: Question answer**
> "Great breakdown! One thing worth knowing — if you own a car, there are credit cards that use your car's equity as collateral instead of your credit score. Completely different from secured cards. Way fewer people know this option exists."

**Template B: State correction**
> "Just to add — availability varies by state. Some of these options aren't in every state so worth checking your specific state first before applying anywhere."

**Template C: Myth bust**
> "The credit score minimum thing trips a lot of people up. Some lenders literally don't look at your score — they look at what collateral you have. Completely different approval criteria."
