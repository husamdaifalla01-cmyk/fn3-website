"Your car is depreciating every day. But there's one thing you can do with it that actually makes it useful for your finances.

There's a credit card that uses your car's equity — not your credit score — to set your limit.

If your car is worth $15,000 and you've paid it off? You could get a credit card with a $7,500 limit. With a 480 credit score.

The Yendo card. It's a real Visa. Builds your credit history. You keep driving your car.

Soft pull to check eligibility — won't affect your score. 36 states. Link in bio."
