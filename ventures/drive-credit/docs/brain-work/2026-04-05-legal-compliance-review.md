### Warnings

**Warning 1: TikTok Video Disclosure**
FTC requires disclosure to be in the VIDEO itself for paid content, not just the caption. Since we're not paying for placement (organic affiliate), caption-only disclosure is acceptable. BUT if we ever do influencer partnerships, the disclosure must be spoken in the video AND in the caption.

**Warning 2: "No Credit Check" Language**
Some scripts use "no credit check" framing. Technically, Yendo does a SOFT pull (which is a credit check, just not a hard inquiry). Recommend changing to:
- "No HARD credit check" or "Soft pull only — doesn't affect your score"
- Avoid: "no credit check" (misleading)
