# Mintbrooks — Week 2 Viral Hook Strategy
**Date:** 2026-04-05 | **Author:** Growth Lead (Dara Denney lens)

## Week 2 Content Strategy (Apr 7-13)
Week 1 = Launch batch. Week 2 = Double down on what works + introduce new angles.

## Hypothesis Framework
We don't know which hook category wins until Week 1 data arrives.
Pre-emptively developing 3 category bets:

### Category A: "System Rigged" (Outrage/Validation)
Hypothesis: Bad credit people feel the system is unfair. Videos validating that feeling + offering a solution will have highest share rate.
- Hook 1: "Banks make $35 billion/year in overdraft fees. From the people who can least afford it."
- Hook 2: "Your credit score doesn't measure if you're trustworthy. It measures if you're profitable to banks."
- Hook 3: "A $200 secured card deposit earns the bank interest. But they call it YOUR deposit."

### Category B: "Did You Know?" (Educational/Curiosity)
Hypothesis: Low-credit audience doesn't know about car-secured products. Pure information wins.
- Hook 1: "If you own a car that's paid off, you have collateral that most banks ignore."
- Hook 2: "36 million Americans own cars with zero loans. Most don't know what that's worth for credit."
- Hook 3: "A $5,000 credit limit with 3% utilization does MORE for your score than a $500 limit at 60% utilization."

### Category C: "Proof/Transformation" (Social Proof)
Hypothesis: Real results beat explanations. Showing the transformation drives the highest clicks.
- Hook 1: "My credit score went from 490 to 580 in 6 months. Here's the only card I used." [requires Husam to film]
- Hook 2: "I applied for 3 credit cards. Denied every time. Then I tried this one." [requires filming]
- Hook 3: "Someone in the comments asked me to document my credit score journey. Starting at 512."

## Viral Loop Design
Goal: Every video should prompt viewers to DO something (not just watch).

| Action | How to prompt |
|--------|--------------|
| Click bio link | "Go check if your car qualifies — takes 30 sec, link in bio" |
| Follow | "I'm documenting this whole journey. Follow me so you don't miss week 2." |
| Comment | "Comment your credit score range and I'll tell you which option fits best" |
| Save | "Save this for when the banks say no again" |
| Share | "Tag someone who's been told they have bad credit" |

## Growth Flywheel (Week 2+ Target)
```
Post video → Views → "Tag someone" prompt
                   ↓
              Comments asking questions
                   ↓
         Reply to comments with pin/link
                   ↓
            Bio link clicks → Yendo leads
                   ↓
             Revenue → Fund paid ads
                   ↓
         Paid reach → More organic reach
```

## TikTok Algorithm Notes
- Videos under 15 seconds get pushed to "completion" — finish rate matters
- Videos with high comment velocity get second push (1-3 days after posting)
- The first 500 views determine if TikTok distributes wider
- Comment-bait questions ("What's your credit score?" "What state are you in?") increase dwell time
