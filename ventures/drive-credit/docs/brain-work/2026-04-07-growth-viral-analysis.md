## Viral Mechanics Available to Us

### 1. TikTok "Stitch" / "Duet" Mechanic
- **Trigger:** Someone stitches our video to add their reaction or experience
- **Virality:** Their audience sees our content + they vouch for it
- **How to encourage:** 
  - End videos with a question: "Has your bank ever rejected you? Stitch this."
  - Respond to comments in video format (triggers algorithm push)
- **Expected time to first organic stitch:** Week 3-4 if we post consistently

### 2. Comment-Bait Hook Style
- **Pattern:** Videos that generate "Is this real?" or "Wait, I didn't know this" comments get algorithmically boosted
- **Our hook:** "Your car is worth $10,000 in credit" — classic disbelief hook
- **Virality trigger:** Comments → algorithm shows to more people → more comments
- **Action:** Respond to EVERY comment in first 24 hours (this is a huge TikTok algorithm signal)

### 3. SEO-to-Social Loop
- Someone searches "how to rebuild credit" → finds our guide → shares on Reddit or Facebook group → traffic spike
- **Reddit moment:** There are active subs: r/personalfinance (18M), r/CRedit (1M), r/povertyfinance (900k)
- **Strategy:** Do NOT spam. Wait until we have 3+ posts + a solid guide. Then organically mention mintbrooks.com when someone asks "how do I rebuild credit with a car?"
- **Timing:** Week 3-4

### 4. Email Word-of-Mouth
- Once email list grows: forward-to-friend CTA in nurture sequence
- "Know someone with bad credit? Forward this."
- **Timing:** Month 2 when list > 50 subscribers
