## Pre-Post Compliance Checklist

### TikTok Requirements (MANDATORY before posting)

- [ ] Video on-screen text includes "#ad" OR "Sponsored" — visible for full video duration
- [ ] Video caption first 3 visible lines include "#ad" or "#sponsored" (before "See more" cut-off)
- [ ] Caption does NOT include "#partner" alone — TikTok requires "#ad" or "#sponsored"
- [ ] No guaranteed approval language ("guaranteed credit card", "guaranteed approval")
- [ ] No "no credit check" language — correct phrasing: "soft inquiry" or "soft pull"
- [ ] "Link in bio" is to mintbrooks.com/links — which already contains FTC disclosure ✅
- [ ] No specific APR or credit limit promises (use "up to $10,000" with disclaimer)

### Instagram Requirements (MANDATORY before posting)

- [ ] Reels: Use Instagram's "Paid Partnership" label if available in creator tools
- [ ] If no Paid Partnership label: "#ad" must appear in first visible line of caption
- [ ] Caption does NOT make bank/lender claims for Mintbrooks
- [ ] Bio link points to mintbrooks.com/links (has FTC affiliate disclosure ✅)

### Caption Compliance Review

**CarEquitySecret caption check:**
> "Your car is worth up to $10,000 in credit you don't know about"
- ✅ "up to" qualifier — not a guarantee
- ✅ #badcredit hashtags — not deceptive
