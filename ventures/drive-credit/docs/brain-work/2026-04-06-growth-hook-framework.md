## Hook Category Performance Prediction (Pre-Launch)

Based on TikTok content patterns in the personal finance vertical, here is the predicted performance ranking before we have data:

### Predicted Hook Rankings (High → Low Engagement)

1. **Social Proof hooks** ("3 people I know got approved") — Script 10
   - Why: Personal stories + outcomes = highest relatability. "People like me" framing.
   - Predicted watch-through: 50–65%

2. **Math/Numbers hooks** ("I did the math on how much my car is worth as credit")

   - Predicted watch-through: 45–60%

3. **Curiosity hooks** ("CarEquitySecret") — Existing render
   - Why: "Secret" framing creates open loop. Car + credit angle is novel.
   - Predicted watch-through: 40–55%
   - Predicted profile click rate: 3–5%

4. **Problem/Pain hooks** ("BankSaysNo") — Existing render
   - Why: Strong emotional resonance but common framing. Less differentiated.
   - Predicted watch-through: 35–50%

5. **Educational hooks** ("CreditSystemBroken") — Existing render
   - Why: Good for saves/shares but lower immediate link clicks.
