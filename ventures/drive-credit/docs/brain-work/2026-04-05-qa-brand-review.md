### Specific Flags
1. **Script 2 ("Why Banks Want You Broke")** — uses "loophole" in caption. Legal flagged this as gray area. Recommend changing to "alternative" or "option."
2. **Hook Variant 2 ("Math Problem")** — uses "utilization" which may lose lower-education-level viewers. Consider: "If you spend half your limit, your score drops."
3. **Pinterest Pin 3** — compares to title loans with "300%+ APR" claim. Need source citation or softened language ("can charge extremely high rates").

### Recommendations
- Create a "banned words" list visible to all content creators
- Minor caption edits to 2 scripts (loophole → alternative, specific APR claim → general)
- Overall quality: STRONG — content is on-brand and compliant
