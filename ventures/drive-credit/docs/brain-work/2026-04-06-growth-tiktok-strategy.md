## TikTok Algorithm — How It Works for a Zero-Follower Account

When you post as a new account, TikTok shows your video to a small test cohort (~200–500 people).
If engagement signals are strong (watch time, likes, shares), it expands to 2k → 10k → 50k+ views.

**The 3-hour window is everything.** If you get 3%+ engagement in the first 3 hours, TikTok pushes it further.

## Hashtag Strategy (Revised for First Video)

### Why Hashtags Matter on TikTok
TikTok uses hashtags primarily for content categorization, not discovery. Over-hashtagging (20+ generic tags) signals spam.

**Best practice:** 3–5 highly relevant tags in the video POST + 10–15 supporting tags in the FIRST COMMENT.
