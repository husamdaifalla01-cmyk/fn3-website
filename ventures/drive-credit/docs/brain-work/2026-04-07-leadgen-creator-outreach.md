## DM Templates

### Option A — Collaboration Pitch (direct but transparent)

```
Hey [Name]! I've been following your content — the way you break down credit myths for regular people is exactly what's missing in this space.

I run Mintbrooks, a site that helps car owners with low credit scores find alternatives to predatory loans. We've got an interesting angle you might not have covered: car-secured credit cards (like Yendo — soft pull, keeps your car, builds credit).

Would you be open to a collab? We can do an honest review format — you keep full editorial control, no scripts. We pay a flat fee + you keep any affiliate revenue from your audience.

DM me if interested or reply here. No pressure either way!
```

**Best for:** Creators with 50K+ followers who already do brand collabs. Shows budget signal early.

### Option B — Organic "I found something interesting" Angle

```
Hey [Name] — quick one. You cover credit rebuilding a lot and I ran into something interesting: car-secured credit cards. Not title loans (which are predatory) — more like a secured card but using your car's equity instead.

Wondering if you've ever heard of this or tested it? We've been covering it at Mintbrooks and your audience demographic seems like a perfect fit for the content. Happy to share our research if useful.
```

**Best for:** Smaller creators (under 50K) or creators who don't typically do paid promos. Leads with curiosity, not commerce.

## Outreach Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Volume | 2-3 DMs per week — not a blast. Personalize each one |
| Warm up | Follow each creator for 3-5 days before DMing. Like 2-3 of their videos first |
| Timing | DM during business hours (not 2am) — shows professionalism |
| Follow up | If no reply after 5 days, one follow-up max. Then move on |
| Tracking | Log each outreach in leadgen state file with date + status |

## Collab Terms Framework

When a creator expresses interest, propose:

- **Format:** Honest review / organic integration (not a scripted ad)
- **Compensation:** Flat fee ($50-$200 depending on audience size) + affiliate revenue share
- **Editorial control:** Full — they can say whatever they actually think
- **Disclosure:** Required — #ad or paid partnership label
- **Content rights:** Creator owns content, Mintbrooks can repost with credit
