**Response:** "We've got you — link in bio has a second option (Slam Dunk Loans) for people without a car. Personal loans up to $50K, any credit history welcome."

### Category 3 — "Does this affect my credit score?"
**Response:** "The eligibility check is a soft pull — zero score impact. Only if you decide to apply does a hard inquiry happen (5–10 points, recovers in a few months). Either way, building credit with the card adds a lot more than those points over time."

### Category 4 — "What states is this available in?"
**Response:** "Yendo is available in 37 states + DC. If you're in one of the 14 excluded states, the link in bio routes you to an alternative option automatically. Check it out and it'll tell you based on your state 👍"

### Category 5 — "I got rejected"
**Response:** "Sorry to hear that — that's frustrating. Yendo has specific eligibility requirements (car ownership is a big one). If you got rejected, the fallback option on the /links page (Slam Dunk Loans) doesn't require collateral. DM us and we can help figure out the best path."

### Category 6 — Positive / "This helped"
**Response:** "Really glad it helped! If you end up trying it, let us know how it goes 💪 drop any questions in the comments, we answer everything."

### Category 7 — Competitor mention ("I use OpenSky / Capital One / etc.")
**Response:** "Great! Those are solid cards too. Yendo is specifically for people who want to skip the cash deposit — if you own a car, your car's equity does that job instead. Different tools for different situations 👍"
