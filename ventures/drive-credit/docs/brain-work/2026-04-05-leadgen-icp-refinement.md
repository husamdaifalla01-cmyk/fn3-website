# ICP Refinement: TikTok Audience Targeting
## Lead Qualifier: 2026-04-05

### Primary ICP Segments (for TikTok content targeting)

**Segment A: "Post-Rejection Rebuilder"** (Highest intent)
- Age: 28-42 | Recently denied credit | Owns paid-off car
- Emotional state: Frustrated, skeptical, ready for alternatives
- Content that converts: Empathy hooks → "The bank said no" scripts
- Estimated % of audience: 35%

**Segment B: "Young Adult, No History"** (Highest volume)
- Age: 22-28 | First car, no credit file | Needs credit for apartments/phones
- Emotional state: Confused, overwhelmed by options
- Content that converts: Education hooks → "3 things I wish I knew" scripts
- Estimated % of audience: 40%

**Segment C: "Bankruptcy/Divorce Recovery"** (Highest urgency)
- Age: 32-50 | Major life event destroyed credit | Owns car with equity
- Emotional state: Rebuilding identity, wants agency
- Content that converts: Redemption stories → "How I started over" scripts
- Estimated % of audience: 15%

**Segment D: "Side Hustle / Gig Worker"** (Emerging)
- Age: 25-35 | 1099 income, thin credit file | Car is their office
- Emotional state: Entrepreneurial, underserved by traditional banks
- Content that converts: Math/logic hooks → "Your car is losing value" scripts
- Estimated % of audience: 10%

### TikTok Targeting Implications
- Hashtags to test: #badcredit #creditrepair #creditbuilding #nocreditcheck #personalfinance #financetok #creditcard #creditscore
- Post timing: 7-9 AM EST (morning scroll), 7-10 PM EST (evening scroll)
- Content mix: 40% empathy, 30% education, 20% urgency, 10% POV/humor
