## Viral Loop: TikTok Comment → Share Trigger

When a TikTok comment asks "does this work for [specific car/situation]?":
1. Reply with specific answer
2. End with: "Tag someone who needs to hear this"
3. Creates organic share loop — each specific reply becomes content for others

**High-trigger comment types to watch for:**
- "My car is 2009, does it qualify?" → "Yes! Yendo accepts vehicles 2001+ in most cases. Tag a friend who has a car sitting in the driveway 👇"
- "I live in Texas, does this work?" → "Yes — Texas is fully covered! Check the link in bio. 🎯"
- "I have 480 credit score" → "That's exactly who this is built for. No credit check to see your limit. 🔑"
