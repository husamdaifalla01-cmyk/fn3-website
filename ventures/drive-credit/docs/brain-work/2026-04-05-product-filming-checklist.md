### Filming Tips for Maximum Algorithm Performance
1. **First 3 seconds are everything** — say the hook line looking directly at camera
2. **No intro** — don't say "hey guys" or "what's up" — jump straight into the hook
3. **Vertical only** — hold phone vertically, frame from chest up
4. **One take is fine** — TikTok rewards authentic over polished
5. **Speak fast but clear** — 150+ words per minute keeps retention
6. **End with CTA** — "Link in bio" + point up gesture

## Key Product Decision: Animated vs. Filmed Content

The single biggest product decision right now is whether to post Remotion-animated videos or wait for filmed content.

**Data points:**
- TikTok algorithm favors consistency over quality in early accounts
- Text-overlay/animation videos DO work on TikTok (many accounts with 100K+ followers use only text animations)
- But face-to-camera gets 2-5x higher watch time on average
- Our Remotion videos are 15s — short enough for high completion rate

**Decision:** Post animated videos NOW. Film content this weekend. Don't let perfect be the enemy of shipped.
