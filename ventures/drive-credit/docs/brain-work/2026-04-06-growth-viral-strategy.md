# Growth — Viral Loop Strategy: First 1,000 Followers
**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Dept:** Growth

---

## The Mintbrooks Growth Problem
Zero followers → zero organic reach → zero leads → zero revenue. This is the cold start problem.

**The algorithm's threshold:** TikTok distributes new videos to ~200–500 test accounts. If watch time > 50% AND swipe-away rate < 30%, it pushes to 2,000–5,000. If that batch performs, it goes wide. 

**The hook is everything.** We need to nail the first 3 seconds.

## Growth Levers by Follower Count

### 0 → 100 followers (next 7 days)
- Post CarEquitySecret + CreditSystemBroken immediately
- Reply to EVERY comment within 1 hour of posting (signals engagement to algorithm)
- Use Stitch/Duet to engage with viral finance content (get discovered by their audience)
- Pin CarEquitySecret as profile pinned video

### 100 → 500 followers (days 7–21)
- Post 3x/week cadence
- Analyze first 2 videos: which had longer average watch time?
- Double down on winning hook style
- Comment on @yendo, @chime, @self.inc posts with value (discovery play)

### 500 → 1,000 followers (days 21–45)
- Unlock TikTok link-in-bio (requires 1,000 — almost there)
- Start responding to comments with mini-videos ("Reply" feature)
- Post "state-specific" content: "If you're in Texas and have a car..." (high specificity = higher share rate)

---

## Compounding Content Strategy

Each video should answer a common objection that becomes the NEXT video's hook:
1. CarEquitySecret → comment: "But does it affect my credit score?" → next video: "I tested it — here's what happened to my score"
2. CreditSystemBroken → comment: "What states?" → next video: "37 states qualify. Is yours one of them?"
3. BankSaysNo → comment: "What's the catch?" → next video: "The catch: you need to own a car"

This is the content compound loop. Comments tell you exactly what to post next.

## KPIs to Track After First Post
- Views at 1h, 6h, 24h (each 10x jump = algorithm signal)
- Average watch time % (target: >50%)
- Follower conversion rate (views → followers, target: >1%)
- Bio link click rate (target: >3% of viewers)
