## Viral Loop #1: "I just got approved" shareable moment

**Trigger:** After Yendo approval, user has a natural "I did it" moment.
**Mechanic:** Add a post-approval share screen on the result-yes page:
```
"🎉 Share your win — help someone else discover this"
[Share to TikTok] [Share to IG] [Copy Link]
```
**Why it works:** Financial milestones (getting approved, getting a credit card) are inherently shareable. Our ICP has been rejected before — approval is a genuine emotional win.

## Growth Experiment: "Refer a Friend" Email Trigger

**Trigger:** Email Day 3 (objection handler) gets decent open rate
**Experiment:** Add P.S. at end of Day 3 email:
```
P.S. — Know someone who owns a car and has bad credit? 
Forward this to them. You might genuinely change their financial situation.
```
**Expected impact:** 5-15% forward rate if the content resonates. Zero cost.

## Week 3 Growth Priority: Creator Amplification Loop

Once 1-2 micro-creators post authentic content:
1. Comment engagement → their followers discover Mintbrooks
2. Creator posts → their audience takes the quiz → approval → creator-attributable conversion
3. Conversion data → recruit more creators ("here's what the first creator earned")

This is the flywheel. The first creator is the wedge.
