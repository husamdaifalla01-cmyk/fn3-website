## Product Rationale

This page targets top-of-funnel users who are afraid to apply for credit. The fear of the hard
inquiry is one of the most common objections to product adoption — if we can defuse that fear
and introduce the soft-pull angle, we convert a hesitant searcher into a Yendo click.

## Conversion Architecture

```
Search: "does applying for credit card hurt credit score"
  → Land on /does-applying-for-credit-card-hurt-credit
    → Learn: hard vs soft, timeline, when to apply
      → CTA: "Check eligibility — soft pull only" → Yendo affiliate link
        → Conversion: MaxBounty lead ($112.50 CPL)
```
