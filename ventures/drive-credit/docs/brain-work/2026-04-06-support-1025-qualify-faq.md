## New Questions to Add to Site FAQ (for /qualify)

### Q: What happens after I take the quiz?
A: The quiz shows you which product you're most likely to qualify for. If you qualify for the Yendo car-secured card, you'll be taken to their site to check your actual eligibility — that check is a soft pull and won't affect your credit score. If your state isn't supported, we'll show you alternative options.

### Q: Why does the quiz ask what state I'm in?
A: The car-secured Visa we recommend (Yendo) is currently available in 37 states + DC. We ask your state so we can point you to the right option — if Yendo isn't available where you are, we'll show you what is.

### Q: What if my state isn't on the list?
A: We'll redirect you to Slam Dunk Loans — a personal loan marketplace that works in all 50 states and is designed for people with bad or no credit. It's a different product (personal loan vs Visa card) but may still help.

### Q: Is the quiz connected to any credit check?
A: No. The quiz itself doesn't connect to any credit bureau or financial system. It's just 3 questions to help us show you the most relevant option. Any actual credit check only happens if you choose to apply through the link we provide.

### Q: Why does it ask about my credit score if the card doesn't check it?
A: Great question — we ask so we can give you context about how strong your likely approval is. Even though Yendo doesn't use your score as the primary approval factor, knowing your credit situation helps us give you more accurate expectations.
