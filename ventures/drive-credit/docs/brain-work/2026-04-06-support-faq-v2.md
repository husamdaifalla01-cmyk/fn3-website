### Q: "What's the credit limit?"
**Response:**
> It depends on your car's equity, not your credit score. The credit limit is typically 50-100% of your car's current value. So a $10K car could get you a $5K-10K credit line. Yendo shows you the exact amount during the soft pull check — no hard pull to see your options.

### Q: "Will this hurt my credit score?"
**Response:**
> The eligibility check is a soft pull — it does NOT affect your credit score at all. Only if you proceed with the full application is there a hard pull. The check itself is totally safe to run.

### Q: "How is this different from a payday loan?"
**Response:**
> Night and day! This is a real Visa credit card, not a payday loan:
> - Payday loans: Short-term, 400%+ APR, due on payday
> - Yendo: Revolving credit card, use it like any Visa, pay over time
> You're using your car as collateral (like a home equity line, but for a car). Way more flexible and cheaper than payday alternatives.

### Q: "What happens if I can't pay?"
**Response:**
