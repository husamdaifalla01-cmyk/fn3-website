## Root Cause Analysis

### Why 37+ Pages Remain Unindexed

**1. Fresh Site (3 weeks old)**
- Deployed to production: 2026-04-13 (3 weeks ago)
- Google's crawl budget is finite; new domains get modest allocation
- Typical indexing latency: 2–4 weeks for 95% coverage on new domains

**2. Canonical & Redirect Issues**
- /finance/calculator has a canonical pointing to /calculator (wrong destination)
- Redirects from old /lifestyle/ paths exist but not all old URLs are gone
- Google treats pages with conflicting canonicals as duplicates (wastes crawl budget)

**3. Internal Linking Gaps**
- Sitemap.ts is correct, but internal nav doesn't link to all finance pages
- Homepage only links to 5 finance pages; 32+ are orphaned
- Google prioritizes crawl based on internal link depth (1–2 hops vs 5+)

**4. Robots.txt / Crawl Blocks**
- verified: robots.txt allows `/finance/*` ✓
- verified: no `noindex` meta tags on finance pages ✓
- verified: sitemap is linked in robots.txt ✓

### Expected Impact of IndexNow

IndexNow bypasses Google's crawl scheduler:
- Files a direct "please crawl this URL" ping to Google's API
- Google treats these URLs as high-priority for the next crawl cycle
- Typical indexing latency after IndexNow: 24–72 hours for discovery, 3–7 days for ranking
