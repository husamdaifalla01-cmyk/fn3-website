How to Choose the Best Hair Gloss or Bonding Oil for Your Damaged Hair

Choosing the right hair gloss or bonding oil for your damaged hair depends on whether you prioritize internal repair, immediate shine, or overnight treatment, and what your budget allows.

When your hair is damaged, you're likely looking for more than just a temporary fix. Consider the specific type of damage you're experiencing. If your hair is chemically damaged or frequently heat-styled, a product with bond-building technology like Olaplex No. 7 is crucial for internal repair. If your primary concern is frizz and a lack of surface shine, then a glossing product like Kerastase Gloss Absolu Glaze Drops or Kenra Platinum Silkening Gloss will deliver immediate cosmetic improvements. For persistent dryness and brittleness, especially if you prefer a 'set it and forget it' approach, an overnight serum like the Kerastase Nutritive 8H Magic Night Serum can provide deep nourishment. Always check the review counts and ratings to see what thousands of other real buyers experienced, and factor in the price point for your regular usage. Our team emphasizes that understanding your hair's specific needs will guide you to the most effective product.

FAQ

Our team answers some common questions you might have about finding the best hair gloss and bonding oils for damaged hair.

What is the best hair gloss for damaged hair?
The best hair gloss for damaged hair that offers both repair and shine is the Olaplex No. 7 Bonding Oil, which uses bond-building technology to strengthen hair while dramatically increasing shine. For purely cosmetic, high-gloss shine, the Kerastase Gloss Absolu Glaze Drops are excellent.
Is Olaplex No. 7 worth it for damaged hair?
Yes, Olaplex No. 7 is absolutely worth it for damaged hair, especially if you have color-treated hair or frequently use heat styling. Its bond-building technology works to repair internal hair structure, not just mask damage, and it provides significant heat protection and frizz control. Over 45,000 reviewers agree with its 4.7-star rating.
Which Kerastase product is best for damaged hair?
For damaged hair, Kerastase offers two excellent options depending on your specific needs: the Kerastase Gloss Absolu Glaze Drops are best if you prioritize immediate 'glass hair' shine and frizz control, while the Kerastase Nutritive 8H Magic Night Serum is best for deep, overnight nourishment and repair of dry and damaged strands.
Can hair gloss repair split ends?
While hair gloss can't permanently repair split ends (only a trim can do that), a good quality gloss or bonding oil, like the Olaplex No. 7, can help to seal the cuticle, making split ends appear smoother and less noticeable, and preventing further damage.
How often should I use a bonding oil on damaged hair?
You can typically use a bonding oil like Olaplex No. 7 daily or every other day, depending on your hair's needs and how often you wash it. Because a little goes a long way, start with a tiny amount and adjust as needed to avoid weighing your hair down.
