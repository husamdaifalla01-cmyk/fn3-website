How to Choose the Right Hair Oil or Serum for Your Damaged Hair

Choosing the best hair oil for your damaged or color-treated hair depends entirely on your specific concerns and desired outcomes, so focus on what your hair truly needs right now.

First, consider the primary issue: Is your hair suffering from internal structural damage (like breakage and split ends from coloring), or is it mostly dry and lacking shine? If you're dealing with structural damage and need actual repair, an oil with bond-building technology, like the Olaplex No. 7 Bonding Oil, should be your priority. This type of product works to mend the hair from within, offering more than just superficial improvements. If your main goal is to add immediate, intense shine and manage frizz for a sleek look, then a glossing serum such as the Kerastase Gloss Absolu Glaze Drops or the more budget-friendly Kenra Platinum Silkening Gloss would be more appropriate. Lastly, if your hair feels chronically dry and parched, particularly overnight, an intensive leave-in treatment like the Kerastase Nutritive 8H Magic Night Serum will provide the deep, sustained hydration it craves. Think about your routine: do you need heat protection for daily styling, or an overnight treatment for deep conditioning? Your choice should align directly with these specific needs.

Key Takeaway: To select the best hair oil, identify your primary hair concern (internal repair, instant shine, or overnight hydration) and choose a product that directly addresses that specific need.
