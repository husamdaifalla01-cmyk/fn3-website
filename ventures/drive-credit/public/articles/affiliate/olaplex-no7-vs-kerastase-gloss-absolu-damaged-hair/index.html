How to Choose the Best Hair Gloss or Oil for Your Damaged Hair

When selecting between a hair gloss and a hair oil for your damaged hair, consider whether you need active repair, instant shine, or overnight nourishment.

Your choice depends on the specific needs of your hair and your priorities. If your hair is severely damaged from color treatments or heat, and you're experiencing breakage, an oil with bond-building technology like Olaplex No. 7 is paramount. This type of product actively works to restore your hair's internal structure. If your hair is damaged but you're primarily seeking a quick, striking visual transformation – that 'glass hair' look – a gloss like Kerastase Gloss Absolu will deliver immediate, high-impact shine. For those with extremely dry, brittle hair that needs consistent, deep conditioning, an overnight treatment such as the Kerastase Nutritive 8H Magic Night Serum will provide sustained nourishment. Always consider your budget; while premium products often offer advanced formulations, effective budget-friendly options like the Kenra Platinum Silkening Gloss are available for surface-level shine and frizz control.

Key Takeaway: Choose a product based on your primary need: bond repair (Olaplex), instant shine (Kerastase Gloss Absolu), or deep overnight nourishment (Kerastase Night Serum).
