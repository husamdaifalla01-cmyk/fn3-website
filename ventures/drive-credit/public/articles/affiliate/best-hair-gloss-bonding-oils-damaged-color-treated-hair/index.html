How to Choose the Best Hair Gloss or Bonding Oil for Your Needs

Choosing the right product for your damaged or color-treated hair depends on your primary concerns and desired outcome. You need to consider whether you prioritize deep repair, intense shine, frizz control, or overnight treatment.

First, identify your hair's main issue: Is it severely damaged from chemical processes, or is it just dull and lacking shine? If repair is paramount, look for products with 'bond-building technology' like Olaplex. If you're chasing that 'glass hair' look, focus on glossing serums designed for high shine. For persistent dryness, an overnight leave-in treatment will provide continuous nourishment. Also, consider your budget; there are effective options at various price points, from the $29.00 Kenra to the $62.00 Kerastase large serum. Always check for heat protection if you frequently style with hot tools, as this is crucial for preventing further damage.
