How to Choose the Right Hair Gloss or Oil for Your Damaged Hair

When you're dealing with damaged hair, selecting the right product means understanding your specific needs—are you prioritizing repair, shine, or a combination of both?

First, consider the primary issue you want to address: if your hair is severely damaged or color-treated, a product with bond-building technology like the Olaplex No. 7 Bonding Oil is crucial for internal repair. If your main goal is to achieve an immediate, high-shine finish, then a dedicated glossing serum like the Kerastase Gloss Absolu Glaze Drops or the more budget-friendly Kenra Platinum Silkening Gloss will deliver those visible results. For those with consistently dry hair that needs deep, sustained nourishment, an overnight treatment such as the Kerastase Nutritive 8H Magic Night Serum can work wonders while you sleep. Always check for features like heat protection if you frequently use hot styling tools, as this is vital for preventing further damage. Finally, read reviewer feedback to see how different hair types respond to the product before making your decision.
