You should typically apply vitamin C in the morning and retinol at night to maximize their individual benefits and avoid potential irritation.

Vitamin C, with its antioxidant properties, is best used in your morning routine to help protect your skin from environmental damage throughout the day. Retinol, which can increase sun sensitivity and is best used when your skin is in repair mode, should be applied as part of your evening skincare regimen.
