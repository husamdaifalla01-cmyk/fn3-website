How to Choose the Best Hair Gloss or Oil for Your Damaged Hair

When selecting the best hair gloss for damaged hair or a reparative oil, you should focus on your specific hair concerns and desired outcomes rather than just buying the most popular product.

Consider if your primary goal is deep repair and strengthening (like with Olaplex), achieving an immediate high-shine 'glass hair' effect (Kerastase Gloss Absolu or Kenra), or an intensive overnight treatment for extreme dryness (Kerastase Nutritive 8H Magic Night Serum). For color-treated hair, look for products that offer bond-building technology and heat protection to safeguard your color and prevent further damage. If budget is a concern, excellent options like Kenra Platinum Silkening Gloss prove you don't always need to spend top dollar for noticeable results. Always check if the product offers heat protection, especially if you frequently use styling tools, as this is crucial for preventing future damage. Finally, read reviewer feedback that speaks to your hair type and damage level to ensure the product aligns with your expectations for shine, frizz control, and overall hair health improvement.
