Do bronzing drops actually work?

How do I use bronzing drops for a natural glow?

To use bronzing drops for a natural glow, you typically mix 1-3 drops with your favorite moisturizer, serum, or foundation in your palm, then apply evenly to your face and neck, adjusting the number of drops to achieve your desired level of warmth and radiance.

Can I use bronzing drops every day?

Yes, you can use bronzing drops like the Glow Recipe Hue Drops every day as part of your regular skincare or makeup routine, as they are designed for daily application to maintain a consistent, customizable glow.

What is the difference between bronzing drops and self-tanner?

The main difference is that bronzing drops provide a temporary, washable tint for a faux glow, while self-tanners contain DHA (dihydroxyacetone) to chemically darken the skin's top layer, resulting in a longer-lasting tan that fades over several days.
