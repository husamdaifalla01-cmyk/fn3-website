How to Choose Desk Accessories

Start with the constraint that's actively annoying you most. Cable clutter? CableBox first. Neck strain from looking down at a laptop? Monitor stand first. Scattered supplies slowing your find-time? Organizer first. Don't optimize a desk that already works — fix the one friction point that interrupts you daily.
