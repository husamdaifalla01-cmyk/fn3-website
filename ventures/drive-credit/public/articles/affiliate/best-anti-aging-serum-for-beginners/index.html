How to Choose Your First Anti-Aging Serum

Choosing your very first anti-aging serum doesn't have to be complicated; focus on your primary skin concern, ingredient compatibility, and your budget to make the best decision.

First, consider what you want to achieve most: Is it to reduce fine lines, brighten dull skin, or tackle dark spots? If fine lines and wrinkles are your main focus, a gentle retinol serum like the La Roche-Posay Pure Retinol Face Serum (4.6 stars, $44.99) is an excellent starting point. If brightening is key, the La Roche-Posay Pure 12% Vitamin C Serum (4.4 stars, $44.99) is a strong contender. For beginners with sensitive skin, always look for dermatologist-tested and fragrance-free formulas to minimize irritation. Finally, setting a budget, like our under-$50 target, helps narrow down options without compromising on quality for your initial steps.
