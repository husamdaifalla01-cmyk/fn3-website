How do I achieve glass hair?

To achieve glass hair, start with clean, well-conditioned hair, apply a high-quality hair gloss or shine serum evenly, then blow-dry your hair smooth using a paddle brush and flat iron for a sleek, reflective finish, setting with a light mist of hairspray if desired.

What's the difference between hair gloss and hair oil?

Hair gloss products are primarily designed to add a reflective, high-shine finish to the hair, often creating a 'glass-like' effect, while hair oils typically focus on nourishment, hydration, and smoothing, though many also contribute to shine.
