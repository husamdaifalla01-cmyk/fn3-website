# /research - Strategic Research & Intelligence

## Description
Deep research capability for market analysis, competitive intelligence, technology evaluation, and trend analysis. Powers the CPO and CTO with real data for decisions.

## Commands

### `/research market [topic]`
Market research and sizing: TAM/SAM/SOM, key players, market gaps, and opportunity scoring.

### `/research competitors [product]`
Competitive analysis: company profile, product strengths/weaknesses, user sentiment, strategic moves, and win strategy.

### `/research tech [technology]`
Technology evaluation: capabilities, benchmarks, comparisons, production users, risks, and CTO recommendation.

### `/research trends [topic]`
Trend analysis: current state, emerging trends, future outlook, and strategic implications.

---

## Research Protocols

1. **Always cite sources** - Link to original research
2. **Recency matters** - Prefer recent data
3. **Multiple sources** - Triangulate information
4. **Bias awareness** - Note potential biases
5. **Actionable output** - End with recommendations
