# FlowNexis3

**Type:** Lean tech holding company
**Structure:** Platform of platforms
**North Star:** 25-person HQ operating system running multiple tech businesses

---

## Identity

FlowNexis3 (FN3) is not an agency, not a venture studio, not a traditional holding company. It's a hybrid built for the AI era — an [[Internal Agency Model]] HQ that serves autonomous [[Business Unit]]s while generating external client revenue from the same capabilities.

The core thesis draws from proven lean holding company models ([[Constellation Software Model]], [[Berkshire Hathaway Model]], [[IAC Spin-Off Playbook]], [[Danaher Business System]], [[Amazon Platform Model]]) and is informed by the [[Anand-Collis Framework]].

- [[DRYJETS]] — on-demand services, contracting phase

## Operating Philosophy

> Logic + Magic — disciplined business controls combined with AI-driven optimization.

Every HQ department operates as an internal agency with [[Transfer Pricing]], [[SLA Framework]], and the [[Amazon API Mandate]] baked in — every service interface designed from the ground up to be externalizable.

The [[FN3 Agent Warehouse]] extends this with an AI workforce: persistent agents with identity, memory, and coding capability running 24/7 operations.

---

## Competitive Moat

[[Bundling Strategy]] — no single competitor offers all six service lines from a unified platform. The closest analogs are fractional executive platforms that broker individual relationships, not integrated service delivery.
